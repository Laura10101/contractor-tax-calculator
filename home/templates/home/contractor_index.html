Welcome to the Contractor Tax Calculator!

IT contractors are highly paid, incredibly busy people who usually have no or little knowledge of tax law, and especially international tax law. Being a demographic who often are able to work remotely, they are often able to benefit from relocating.

This calculator is of use to any IT contractor considering moving countries. The goal of this calculator is to allow them to see an indicative calculation of# how a move would affect their take home pay, without them having to spend weeks investigating complex legal issues or spending thousands on tax advisers.

To use the calculator, all you need to do is:

  1. Register for an account (or login if you already have one)
  2. Select the jurisdictions to consider
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to enter your financial information
  4. View your calculation
